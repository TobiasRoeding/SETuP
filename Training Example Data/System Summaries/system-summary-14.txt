Woodward and Bernstein have said they will not reveal the identity of "Deep Throat" until his death. Washington Post reporters Bob Woodward and Carl Bernstein confirmed Tuesday that former FBI number-two W. Mark Felt was the "Deep Throat" source who helped unravel the Watergate scandal and bring down President Richard Nixon. Patrick Gray, to the post. In the 2002 book "The Bureau: The Secret History of the FBI," another former Post reporter, Ronald Kessler, wrote that Deep Throat was probably Felt. Some journalists did divine that Felt was Deep Throat, including James Mann, a former Los Angeles Times correspondent.
