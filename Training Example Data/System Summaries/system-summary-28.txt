Aides to the late president Richard Nixon said Wednesday that former FBI deputy director Mark Felt, unmasked as the anonymous Watergate source known as "Deep Throat," had breached professional ethics by leaking information. Bob Woodward's book on "Deep Throat" will be rushed onto bookshelves by next month, his publisher said Thursday, two days after a monthly magazine revealed the famed Watergate source's real identity. Deep Throat, the secret source whose insider guidance was vital to The Washington Post's groundbreaking coverage of the Watergate scandal, was a pillar of the FBI named W. Mark Felt, The Post confirmed.
