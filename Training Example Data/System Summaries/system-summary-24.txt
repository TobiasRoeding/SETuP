Washington Post reporters Bob Woodward and Carl Bernstein confirmed Tuesday that former FBI number-two W. Mark Felt was the "Deep Throat" source who helped unravel the Watergate scandal and bring down President Richard Nixon. A retired FBI official, Felt, was the "Deep Throat" source who leaked secrets to two Washington Post reporters about the Watergate scandal that brought down former president Nixon. Aides to the late president Nixon said Wednesday that former FBI deputy director Felt, unmasked as the anonymous Watergate source known as "Deep Throat," had breached professional ethics by leaking information.
